Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zee Tech Innovations ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the website zeeshanapps.com and the Android applications we publish, including Puzzle Game and Music Player (together, the "Apps").

Information we collect

Our website and apps are built to require as little personal data as possible. Depending on the app, the following categories of information may be processed:

  • Locally stored data — such as game progress, saved playlists, or favourites, which is stored on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Advertising identifiers — our apps display ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, subject to Google's own privacy policy.
  • Website analytics — standard, aggregated web traffic data (such as pages visited and general location by country) may be collected when you visit this website.

How we use information

Any information collected is used only to operate and improve our apps and website, and to support ad-based monetization through AdMob. We do not sell personal information, and we do not use collected data for any purpose beyond app functionality, analytics, and advertising.

Third-party services

Our apps use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Ads Policies. We do not control how Google processes this data beyond the configuration options AdMob provides to publishers.

Children's privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Where an app's content rating indicates a broader audience, ad serving is configured in line with Google's applicable child-directed treatment requirements.

Data retention and security

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app. We take reasonable measures to protect any information we do process, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You can limit ad personalization through your device's ad settings (for example, Android's "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting), and you can clear locally stored app data at any time through your device's app settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent through our contact page.
